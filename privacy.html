Privacy Policy

schedule

Last Updated: December 21, 2025

BondBoost does not upload user's contacts to any server. The app is designed to work exclusively with the user's local contact data on their device. This approach ensures user privacy and data protection. No contact information is sent, shared, or stored on any external services or applications. The sole purpose of BondBoost is to help users rekindle their connections with friends, family, and acquaintances.

At BondBoost, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we protect your personal information when you use the BondBoost app ("App") on your iOS device.

Information We Do Not Collect

BondBoost does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information to any third-party servers. The App is designed to operate solely on your local device, and no access is granted beyond this environment.

Information We Access

To suggest a contact for you to make a connection with, the App requires permission to access your contact list stored on your local device. By granting this permission, you allow the App to randomly go through your contacts and make suggestions. We do not collect or store any of this information.

Data Security

Since the App does not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no risk of your data being compromised in transit or on third-party servers. Your contact list remains securely stored on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any changes to this policy, we will notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Have questions about your privacy?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us. We usually respond within 24 hours.
