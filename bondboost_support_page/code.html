Common Solutions

Check these frequently asked questions before contacting support.

How do I reset my password?

expand_more

Go to the login screen and tap "Forgot Password". We'll send you an email with instructions to create a new password securely.

Why aren't my contacts syncing?

expand_more

Ensure you have granted BondBoost permission to access your contacts in your phone settings. Try pulling down on the list to refresh.

The app keeps crashing, what do I do?

expand_more

Please ensure you are using the latest version of the app. Try clearing the cache or reinstalling the app if the problem persists.

How to cancel my subscription?

expand_more

You can manage your subscription directly through your Apple ID or Google Play Store settings under "Subscriptions".
