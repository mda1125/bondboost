General Privacy Data Account

What exactly does BondBoost do?

expand_more

BondBoost is a relationship management tool designed to help you deepen your connections. It acts as a personal CRM for your personal life, allowing you to track important dates, shared memories, gift ideas, and check-in frequencies for your friends, family, and partner.

Is the app free to use?

expand_more

Yes, BondBoost offers a robust free tier that includes unlimited contacts and basic reminders. We also offer a Premium subscription called "BondBoost+" which unlocks advanced analytics, cloud sync across multiple devices, and AI-powered gift suggestions.

Is my relationship data private?

expand_more

Absolutely. Privacy is our core value. By default, all your personal notes and relationship details are stored locally on your device using 256-bit encryption. If you choose to enable cloud sync, your data is end-to-end encrypted before it ever leaves your phone, meaning not even our team can read your personal notes.

How do I export my data?

expand_more

You can export your data at any time from the Settings menu. We support CSV and JSON export formats, allowing you to easily transfer your relationship data to other tools or keep a personal backup on your computer.

Do you sell my data to third parties?

expand_more

No. Never. We are funded by user subscriptions, not by advertising. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or third parties. Your relationships are your business, not ours.
